We intend to organize a Scripps SWOT discussion group that will meet monthly. Each meeting will last for one to one and half hours, featuring presentations from one or two researchers reporting their progress on SWOT-related research. These meetings will provide an informal setting for researchers to share their work, receive feedback, and foster collaborations. This will be discussed at the workshop on July 29th, 2024.
